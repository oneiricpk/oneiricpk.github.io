Attitudes and Beliefs

Our corporate philosophies, attitudes and beliefs are known to all team members. They are on it as their habit. This separates us from our competitors as we are more in-tune with our clients’ daily needs resulting in continued service satisfaction.

Our Philosophy

Our philosophy is reflective in many key areas such as:

  • Continued communication with key personnel on-site.
  • Implementing, monitoring and reviewing of standards and quality control procedures.
  • Providing Support in the continued development and evolution of the site.
  • The ability for any client, employee or supplier to be able to speak directly to the decision-maker regarding any factors relating to personnel or site matters.

Our Mission

Oneiric Int’l provides leading edge solutions to the specific needs of its client. Continuous improvement, consistent quality, expert fabrications and healthy food supply to our clients is our hallmark.

Perfection levels are assured through commitment and strict adherence to prevalent international standards. We are on towards steady growth to benefit our client, our employees and our company.

Our Vision

We are determined to continually meet the needs of our clients in an effective, efficient and proactive way. We like to work in conjunction with our clients allowing us to provide suggestions, solutions and professional advice on how best we can facilitate their needs.

In order to achieve this, the company advocates a personalized and professional approach with our existing and future clientele. We believe that the strength of our obligations is fundamental to the growth and success of the business.

Our Values

To be the best service providers, we place extra high focus on our values and the key factors are:
  • Delivering in time
  • Serving beyond expectation
  • Attempting always to be innovative
  • Presenting hygienic and healthy food
  • Passionate of quality
  • Always following a Can Do attitude
  • Win through teamwork
  • Build long term client relationship
  • Prompt action on clients inputs and inquires
  • Respecting environment, health and safety
