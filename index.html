Our recent deliveries to end users

In a very short span of time from inception, Oneiric, by the grace of Almighty Allah swt, has served a good number of clients covering diverse nature of jobs; a few of them are featured here.

Folding Porta Cabins

January 15, 2016

Oneiric is blessed with a milestone as being pioneer of developing and assembling the very first time in Pakistan, Folding Portable Cabins. These were imported by one of its client from China in panel form however the steel structure was also imported as girders and channel. Our engineers and professional team of fabricators converted those raw materials into an eye catching reality.

Re-location of Light Structure Building

February 10, 2016

For one of our client serving at wind energy farms located in Sind province, Oneiric shifted a light structure building for 20 person’s senior accommodation from Gharo to Jhampir. This is a mark of excellence that we achieved in just 30 working days.

20 Feet Washroom Cabins

March 1, 2016

For one of our Chinese client working at the ports of Karachi, we have recently fabricated at our own facility a 20 feet washroom porta-cabin with four separate toilets and other facilities inside.
